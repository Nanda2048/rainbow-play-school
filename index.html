✨ About Us

At Rainbow Play School, we believe every child is unique and deserves a joyful learning environment. Our school focuses on nurturing creativity, confidence, and curiosity in young minds. We teach alphabets, numbers, and abacus basics through fun and interactive activities. Children learn through play, storytelling, and hands-on experiences. We provide a safe, caring, and engaging atmosphere where every child feels happy and secure. Our goal is to build a strong foundation for your child’s future learning journey.
