About Us

At Rainbow Play School, we believe every child is unique and deserves a joyful learning environment. We teach alphabets, numbers, and abacus basics through fun activities. Children learn through storytelling, games, and hands-on interaction. We provide a safe, caring environment where kids feel happy and confident. Our goal is to build a strong foundation for their bright future.
